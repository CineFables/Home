ANNOUNCEMENT!

Dear valued users,
We regret to inform you that "CineFables" has made the difficult decision to cease operations and permanently close our website. This decision stems from internal conflicts and careful considerations of operational costs. We sincerely appreciate the support and engagement from our users throughout the years. Unfortunately, the challenges we are facing internally have led us to this unavoidable conclusion. The closure will be effective on February 15, 2024 and the website will no longer be accessible thereafter. We understand the impact this may have on our users, and we apologize for any inconvenience caused. We want to express our gratitude for your trust and loyalty.
Thank you for being a part of CineFables.com

Sincerely
Xenoid & Team
